LOCUS: National Technical Exhibition 2023

LOCUS is the biggest Technical Fest in Nepal. It has a long history of 20 years. LOCUS is an umbrella organization led by undergraduate students of Electrical, Electronics, and Computer Engineering departments from IOE, Pulchowk Campus. All activities are guided by a single compass: fostering collaboration. Let's celebrate this March on the name of new innovation!
